pmcThe word "Bronchoalveolar" is misspelled in the article title. The correct title is: Comparison of the bronchoalveolar lavage fluid proteomics between foals and adult horses. The correct citation is: Rivolta AA, Bujold AR, Wilmarth PA, Phinney BS, Navelski JP, Horohov DW, et al. (2023) Comparison of the bronchoalveolar lavage fluid proteomics between foals and adult horses. PLoS ONE 18(9): e0290778. https://doi.org/10.1371/journal.pone.0290778.
